About the Sabha

Sri Thyagabrahma Sabha, Thanjavur is devoted to the promotion of Carnatic music and the classical arts, in the very region where the Trinity of Carnatic music — Saint Thyagaraja, Muthuswami Dikshitar and Syama Sastri — lived and composed.

Through the decades, the Sabha has served as a stage for legendary vidwans and promising young artistes alike, presenting vocal and instrumental concerts, Bharatanatyam recitals, harikatha and annual festivals for the rasikas of Thanjavur.

The Sabha remains grateful to its patrons, well-wishers and members — the pillars of strength who have sustained its activities year after year and made every festival and celebration possible.

Platinum Jubilee

The Sabha proudly celebrated its Platinum Jubilee, marking seventy-five years of service to Carnatic music. A commemorative souvenir (malar) was released on the occasion, made rich by the contributions of advertisers, patrons and members of the Sabha.

Our Tradition

Sabha

An assembly of musicians and rasikas in the context of Carnatic music — the living forum where artiste and audience meet, and where the art is nurtured from one generation to the next.

Bharatanatyam

The classical dance form that took shape in Tamil Nadu — an art of rhythm, expression and devotion that the Sabha presents alongside music in its programmes each year.

Music Festival

Multi-day celebrations of concerts by vidwans, vocalists and ensembles — the high point of the Sabha's calendar, honouring Saint Thyagaraja and the Carnatic tradition.

Programmes & Festivals

January

Sri Thyagaraja Aradhana

Homage to Saint Thyagaraja with the rendering of the Pancharatna kritis and concerts by invited artistes, observed in the season of the annual Aradhana.

Annual

Music Festival

The Sabha's flagship festival — several days of vocal and instrumental concerts featuring senior vidwans and talented young performers.

Year-round

Monthly Concerts & Dance Recitals

Regular kutcheris, Bharatanatyam recitals and harikatha programmes for the rasikas of Thanjavur, with a special place for emerging artistes.
